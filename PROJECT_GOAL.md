## 🌱 Climate Crop AI
**Project Goal:** To empower farmers with AI-driven agricultural intelligence, helping them make data-backed decisions on the most resilient crops to plant for the upcoming season. Built during a 2025 hackathon, this web application analyzes historical weather data, soil conditions, and upcoming season characteristics to calculate a "Climate Risk Score" and recommend the best crops.

**Key Features:**
- **AI Crop Prediction Algorithm:** Evaluates crops based on rainfall, temperature, soil, and season.
- **Climate Risk Assessment:** Provides a visual 0-100 risk score and yield stability metrics.
- **Interactive Data Visualization:** Includes multiple chart types (Radar, Bar, Line) and a comprehensive comparison table.
- **Modern User Interface:** Features a full glassmorphism design with responsive dark/light modes.
- **Reporting:** One-click PDF report generation.

**Tech Stack:** Python, Flask, Custom Scoring Logic, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), Chart.js, TailwindCSS.
